# Strategic-Roadmap-for-Scaling-CCUS-in-the-EU
This strategic roadmap translates the Joint Research Centre's 2024 Clean Energy Technology Observatory findings into a phased, actionable plan for scaling CCUS across the EU's hard-to-abate sectors — cement, steel, and chemicals.

Core Objectives
What This Project Delivers
Actionable Strategy
Move beyond high-level observations to a concrete, de-risked implementation plan for CCUS deployment across the EU's industrial heartland.

Evidence-Based
Built on the latest JRC/CETO 2024 findings, patent landscape analysis, and real-world project pipeline data from across the European Union.

Multi-Stakeholder
Integrating perspectives from policymakers, industry leaders, financial institutions, technology providers, and civil society organisations.

EU-Wide Scope
Covering all 27 member states with attention to regional differences in industrial clusters, geological storage potential, and policy readiness.

Methodology
Research Approach
Desk research building on JRC/CETO 2024 report and EU regulatory framework
Expert interviews from industry, finance, and policy domains
Patent and innovation landscape analysis for emerging CCUS technologies
Financial modelling of investment gaps and de-risking mechanisms
Stakeholder mapping and governance framework design
Geographic Focus
EU-27, with consideration for regional differences in industrial clusters and geological storage potential across member states.

Evaluation
Success Criteria
Actionable
Clear steps and milestones, not just analysis

Realistic
Acknowledges and plans for barriers identified by the JRC

Comprehensive
Integrates technical, economic, and policy dimensions

Aligned
Consistent with Net-Zero Industry Act and EU climate strategy
